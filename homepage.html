About Mark

Mark Llobrera is a web developer with experience building everything from responsive web sites to touchscreen applications. He has been working on the web since the days of squelchy modem noises and spacer GIFs, and his work has been recognized by The FWA, D&AD, Adobe, The Webbys, and Cannes Lions. He received his B.A. from Vassar College in 1999, and he is currently a Senior Interactive Developer with Bluecadet in Philadelphia.
